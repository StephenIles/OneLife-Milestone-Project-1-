Community Events

As a community we run events every week for all, ranging from karaoke night, movies nights, game nights, art nights or anything that you would like to be able to share with the rest of the community. We are also open to new event ideas and encourage people who would like to host an event to come and speak to the admin team and we will work with you to get an event sorted.
