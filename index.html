Features

Weekly Community Events

With our ever expanding community of people from all over the world all with different interests, we want to encourage people to explore and join in with activies that they enjoy and are passionate about. To help with this we run weekly event for anything Gaming, Coding, Art Events, Karaoke, Movie Nights and so much more. You can see what events are running on our Community Page.

Friendly Admin Team

To make sure everyone feels welcome and safe in our community we have enlisted the help of some great Mods and Admins to be around to help with any problems. All staff are all very approachable and you will always find some staff online for any issues that you have. Of course, if they do not answer straight away you can open a ticket or use the Question Form to ask any questions that you need.

25+ Mature Server

We have made the decision to make this server 25+ server as we felt this helps keep the feel of the community we are looking to build for you. We do make exceptions and if you are under 25 please use the Question Form to send us a message and a Staff member will get back to you.
